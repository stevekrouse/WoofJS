When is my student ready for WoofJS?

Before coding in WoofJS, we recommend students are very comfortable in Scratch. If they can create a game similar to this on their own, they are defintely ready for WoofJS!

How to teach WoofJS

Project Based Learning

Teaching WoofJS is a wonderful way to allow your students to explore their creativity, take on difficult challenges, and build critical thinking skills. We have a number of projects that your students can begin coding with to familiarize themselves with WoofJS and then you can allow them to work independently. As a teacher, you can support their learning through asking them Socratic Questions.
